Electronics and Communication

Electronics and Communication Engineering is a leading branch of engineering. Every recent trend and new development consists of something that finds its roots in electronics and communication. Electronics and Communications Engineering involves researching, designing, developing and testing of electronic equipment used in various systems. Electronics and Communications engineers also conceptualize and oversee the manufacturing of communications and broadcast systems. It is a Branch of Engineering in which you can learn both hardware and software as well as there are many job opportunities in government sector and private sector.

Information Technology

The Information technology branch is the pioneer branch for all the technical fields.IT branch offers a variety of subjects focusing on root level installation ,implementing the tools and algorithms,designing and debugging the computer system as well as applications. The IT department of Ahmedabad Institute of Technology offers core subjects along with elective subjects to complement the career advancement in software as well as hardware engineering. The major areas of information technology focuses on database management, networking, security, data mining, cyber security, technical compatibilities along with creative innovations to eliminate the research gap.

Mechanical Engineering

One of the oldest and broadest engineering branch, Mechanical Engineering involves designing, development, production and use of mechanical tools to solve mechanical issues. Generally hired by manufacturing industries, these engineers have employment opportunity in both private and public sectors. Government sectors like Defense, PWD, Post and Telegraphs, and CPWD also hire mechanical engineers. Ahmedabad Institute Of Technology’s Department of Mechanical Engineering was established in 2004, which offer “Bachelor of Engineering”. The department runs one post graduate “Master of Engineering in” since 2012 under Gujarat Technological University.
